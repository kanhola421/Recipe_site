Chocolate cake recipe

Here is a fast and easy version of a chocolate cake recipe. Enjoy!!!


DifficultyPreparationCookingTotal time
Easy10 min15 min25 min

Ingrdients

Black chocolate: 200g

Sugar: 150g

Butter: 200g

Eggs: 4

Flour: 80g

Instructions

1

Mix the chocolate and the butter together

2

Mix the sugar, eggs and flour

3

Mix everthing to get a smooth dough

4

Melt the chocolateand the butter together

5

Bake it all in the oven
